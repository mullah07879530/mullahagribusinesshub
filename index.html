Nutri Porridge Product

Ifu y'igikoma cy'umwimerere kandi gikize ku ntungamubiri zikomeye. Ni ifu nziza cyane yagenewe gufasha Abana gukura neza, ifasha abamama bonsa kongera amashereka, ndetse n'abantu bakuru ibafasha gukomeza kugira amagara mazima n'imbaraga.

🌾

Ibiryo by'Amatungo (Feeds)

Shaka ibiryo binoze kandi bifite intungamubiri zuzuye z'inkoko (Layers & Broilers), ingurube, n'inka z'amata mu bice byose.

🩺

Ubuvuzi bw'Amatungo (Veterinary)

Abavuzi b'amatungo bafite ubunararibonye biteguye kuza kuvura, gukingira, no gukurikirana ubuzima bw'amatungo yawe muburyo bwa kinyamwuga.

📊

Inama n'Imishinga (Consultancy)

Tugufasha gukora inyigo z'imishinga y'ubuhinzi n'ubworozi (Poultry, Dairy & Pigs), gutunganya ibyuzi by'amafi, n'amahugurwa y'abakozi.
